Giuseppe Baldini

I am a Space Industry Analyst at Beijing Commsat Technology, a Chinese private space company. My position revolves around quantitative industry research with the aim to identify commercial applications for the company proprietary satellite constellation.

I graduated from the Global Masters in Management, a double degree programme offered by London Business School and Fudan University. During my time in Shanghai, I launched the first Singularity University Chapter in mainland China and completed my Masters thesis on adoption factors of robotic technology in manufacturing.

Prior to starting my academic year in Shanghai, I spent the summer working for Seeder Clean Energy, researching blockchain applications for the energy sector and starting the implementation of a Renewable Energy Credits (RECs) program.
During the first academic year in London I led Product Parterships at BLOCKS Wearables, the company behind the world's first modular smartwatch.

I hold a Bachelor's in Management from Bocconi University, with an exchange semester in Hanyang University (Seoul, South Korea) and a Business Development internship in a Milan based AI startup, Occambee.

I am interested in technology with the potential to benefit humanity in the long term and I am currently trying to get better at programming in Python, communicating in Chinese and playing the ocarina.
